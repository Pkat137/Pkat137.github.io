F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What languages do you specialize in for translation?

    I primarily translate from Chinese to Hindi and English. However, I can also offer translation services in a variety of languages. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding your special language requirements.

  • How do you ensure the accuracy of your translations?

    Accuracy is really important to me. I am a native speaker of the languages with which I deal. To achieve the greatest degree of accuracy in my translations, I employ modern translation technologies, undertake extensive research, and pay close attention to language subtleties.

  • Can you handle sensitive or confidential documents?

    Yes, I recognise the significance of secrecy in translation work. All documents are treated with the highest secrecy and respect by me. If necessary, I am willing to sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) to protect the confidentiality and security of your sensitive information.

  • What types of content do you translate?

    I am capable of translating a wide range of information, including but not limited to website content, marketing materials, legal papers, technical manuals, personal letters, and other things. I am capable of handling a wide range of information, whether business-related or personal.

  • How can I request a translation or get a quote for my project?

    It's simple to get started! Simply contact me using the contact information provided on the website or fill out the contact form. Please describe your project in full, including the source and target languages, the volume of content, and any special needs. I will swiftly respond with more information and a quote.
