F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What languages do you offer translation and interpretation services in?

    We provide services in all major languages throughout the world, including but not limited to French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Italian, Portuguese, and Arabic. If you have unique linguistic requirements, please contact us and we will try our best to meet them.

  • How do I request a quote for translation or interpretation services?

    Requesting a quotation is simple and quick! Simply go to our website and click on the "Contact Us" link. Fill in the blanks with personal information, such as the Name, Email address, Subject, and other necessary information like language pair, document type, and any special instructions in the Message Field. We will promptly provide you with a detailed quote based on your requirements.

  • Can you provide interpretation services for business meetings or events?

    Absolutely! We have professional interpreters that specialise in a variety of sectors, such as business meetings, conferences, and seminars. We will work together with you to provide flawless communication during your event, whether you require sequential or simultaneous interpretation.

  • What types of content do you translate?

    We are capable of translating a wide range of information, including but not limited to website content, marketing materials, legal papers, technical manuals, personal letters, and other things. We are capable of handling a wide range of information, whether business-related or personal.

  • Is my information and documents kept confidential?

    We place a high value on confidentiality. To safeguard the security of your information and documents, we have strong privacy policies in place. Our crew follows rigorous confidentiality agreements and treats your data with the utmost caution and care.

  • What forms of payment do you accept?

    We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal as payment methods. Payment choices will be detailed in the quotation and invoice that you receive.

  • What if I have specific formatting requirements for my translated documents?

    We recognise that formatting is essential for some publications. Please supply us with any precise formatting needs, such as font style, size, and layout, and we will make every effort to guarantee that the translated documents closely follow your preferred formatting.
